About Us

We consider ourselves as your partner and not as supplier, because we take pride in what we do, we are passionate about it and because your success – is our success. Our print manufacturing facility is located in the beautiful Okanagan Valley of British Columbia. With such a strategic location we are able to deliver high quality printing and design services to businesses and consumers in Kelowna, Penticton, Vernon, Kamloops, and Okanagan throughout British Columbia and all over Canada. Give us a call if you have any questions or to book an appointment or to request a sample pack. We are always happy to assist you with any of your printing and design needs.

MR PRINT is your one stop print shop.
