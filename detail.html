    GAGADONNA by Janet K.

    Gagadonna is a special pop art piece that was illustrated by one of the cutting pop artist Janet K.. Her unique use of colors assign vividness and impact into the illustration and the rough brush strokes creates emphasis over the character. Well the feature of Janet's work is hybridization of famous celebrities, especially in this piece, Madonna and Lady Gaga. Her strength is to extract unique characteristics of the celebrities and harmoniously combine it into one.

    This is a fun take on a modern social trend. Minimalistic solid background and the texture of the illustration help to creates more sensual mood over the piece. Emphasis is put on the character’s personalities and the gestural behaviour. Thie symbolic posture of Madonna adds more fun to the piece.
