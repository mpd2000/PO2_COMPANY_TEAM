About Us

Looking for a print shop or printing company that can handle all of you printing requirements? Welcome to Print Factory where our wide range of printing services, design services and outstanding quality meet our friendly and knowledgeable staff ready to assist you with all your design and printing needs. Printed materials make a very strong and lasting impact on your business and we'd love to show you options are available to help you promote your business, organization or event. Our product Specialists can help your business by matching up the right product to get the desired message across.

We consider ourselves as your partner and not as supplier, because we take pride in what we do, we are passionate about it and because your success – is our success. Our print manufacturing facility is located in the beautiful Okanagan Valley of British Columbia. With such a strategic location we are able to deliver high quality printing and design services to businesses and consumers in Kelowna, Penticton, Vernon, Kamloops, and Okanagan throughout British Columbia and all over Canada. Give us a call if you have any questions or to book an appointment or to request a sample pack. We are always happy to assist you with any of your printing and design needs. Print Factory is your one stop print shop or printing company.
